Layout Tips

Get the panels right

  • 🖼️Cover firstPanel 1 is your title page — make it grab attention.
  • 🔳Cover vs contain"Cover" fills the panel (may crop); "contain" shows the whole image.
  • 🔤Keep captions shortPanels are small — a few words read better than a paragraph.
  • ↔️Drag to re-sequenceStory order matters; rearrange until it reads like a booklet.
